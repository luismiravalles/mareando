What is My Tide

  • My Tyde aims to show the state of the tide in the most friendly way possible
  • No internet connection needed
  • You can add your favourite location. My Tyde will show the nearest data
  • And the best: You can take photos of your place at any time and each photo will be registered with actual height of the tide, so next time you consult your location, you will see the photo wich is much more about actual height.
  • Without ads!!
